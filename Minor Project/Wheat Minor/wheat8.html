Apply basal fertilizers at sowing

week 1

• For irrigated wheat, apply 51 kg DAP, 32 kg urea and 28 kg of muriate of potash (MOP) per acre.
• For rainfed wheat, apply 22 kg of DAP, 9 kg of urea and 14 kg of MOP per acre.
• To avoid risk of poor germination due to direct contact between seed and fertilizers, place your fertilizer at least 4-6 cm away from the seed.
• Use a seed-cum-fertilizer drill, which sows the seeds and places fertilizer next to the seed at the same time. This land machinery helps you to apply seeds and fertilizers in separated columns or furrows.

Time to apply fertilizer

week 4

• Apply 52 kg per acre of urea 20-25 days after sowing.
• Irrigate field after application of fertilizer.
• Broadcast the fertilizer if no fertilizer drill is available.
• Reduce the nitrogen dose to one half if the field has been green manured in the preceding kharif season.

Foliar application of Nitrogen

week 8

Foliar application of 5% urea and KCI solution at 50, 70 and 85 days intervals after sowing is advantageous to improve dry matter accumulation and net assimilation rate in wheat.
