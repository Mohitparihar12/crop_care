When to harvest

week 16

• Harvest wheat at physiological maturity when the grain moisture is around 15%.
• crop is harvested when the grains become hard and the straw becomes dry.
• The harvesting stage normally comes when the plant turns golden yellow and becomes brittle.
• Harvesting is mostly done by sickle.
• The crop is threshed by treading with cattle on the threshing floor or by a power-driven thresher.
• By using a combine harvester, the harvesting and threshing operations can be carried out simultaneously.
