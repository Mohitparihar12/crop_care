Weed management

Week 2

Here are some recommendations on proper weed management:
• Keep bed weed-free during the early stages of growth.
• Carry out intercultural operations 15-20 days after sowing.
• Depending on the severity and intensity of weeds, weed your fields approximately once a month.
• Later on as the vines begin to spread, less weeding is necessary.
• The use of herbicides must be done very carefully or else the healthy plants may be damaged.
• When weeds are not kept under control, they can cause yield loss up to 30%.
