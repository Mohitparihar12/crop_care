Furrow,pit and hill planting methods

Week 1


Seed rate: For one acre, use 600 to 800 grams of seeds.

Watermelon seeds can be sown in different ways:
• Furrow method: Create furrows 2-3 meters apart. Sow 3-4 seeds at a time and keep a plant to plant distance of 60-90cm. Sowing is done on either side of furrows. After germination, keep only one seedling.
• Pit method: Create pits of 60x60x60 cm. Keep a distance of 2-3.5 meters between two rows and 60 cm to 1.2 meters between pits. Fill the pits with well-decomposed farmyard manure and soil. Sow 4 seeds in each pit. After germination, keep only one seedling.
• Hill method: Create hills of 30x30x30cm. Keep a distance of 1-1.5 meters between hills. Sow two seeds per hill. After germination, keep only one seedling.
