Week 18


Two main observations are used to determine the correct harvest time for rice grains:
• Moisture content: Grain moisture content is ideally between 18% and 22% w.b. (wet basis). Test the grains between your teeth. They should be firm, but not crumbly.
• Ripe grains per panicle: The crop should be harvested when 80-85% of the grains on the field have changed color from green to matured yellow.
