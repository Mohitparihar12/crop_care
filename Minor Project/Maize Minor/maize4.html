Good field condition for your maize crop

3 Weeks before seedling

Soils with good organic matter content and high water-holding capacity with neutral pH are considered good for higher productivity. Being a sensitive crop to moisture stress, particularly excess soil moisture and salinity stresses, it is desirable to avoid low-lying fields having poor drainage and also the field having a higher salinity. Therefore, fields with provisions for proper drainage should be selected for the cultivation of maize.
