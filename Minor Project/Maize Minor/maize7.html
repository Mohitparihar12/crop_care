Irrigation your crop regularly

Weeks 2

In regions with about 60cm of well-distributed rainfall during the growing seasons,additional irrigation is not necessary.
• The total amount of irrigation will depend on the rainfall distribution pattern.
• The spring and rabi crops are entirely raised under irrigation. The amount of irrigation varies.
• For winter maize, it is advisable to keep the soil wet (frequent & mild irrigation) from 15 December to 15 February to protect the crop from frost injury.
• Irrigate the field just up to saturation level and do not flood the field.
• In general, the irrigation should be applied in furrows up to the 2/3rd height of the ridges/beds.
• Irrigate the field with about 5-7.5cm.
• Irrigation is done in the furrow between the two rows of plants.
• Irrigate before each of the two top dressings.
• The critical times for irrigation are the knee hight stage (V8), the tasseling stage, silk initiation in the cob and the milking stage of the grain.

Water your plants during flowering

week-9

• Flowering and post-flowering are the most susceptible stages for moisture stress.
• Irrigate your crop with approximately 5-7.5 cm of water.
• Irrigate again if there is inadequate soil moisture during the flowering and post-flowering (i.e. grain filling) stages.
