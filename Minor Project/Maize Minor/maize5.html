Be precise with primary field preparation

3 Weeks before seedling

Maize requires a fine and tight seedbed free from weeds. There are several steps that you need to take for a fine seedbed:
• One deep ploughing to the depth of 15-20 cm.
• Clod crushing.
• Stubble collection.
• Harrowing twice.
• Before the last harrowing, add well-composed farmyard manure or ripe compost in the field and mix well while harrowing.
• The ridges and furrows are opened at a distance of 60 cm to 75 cm for grain crops in medium to heavy soils, respectively.
